Ganesh chaturthi


Ganesh Chaturthi is a festival that pays homage to Lord Ganesh,the remover of obstacles,and celebrates the day of his birth. Many recognize that Lord Ganesh brings order in this universe and worship him before embarking on a new endeavor and intellectual journey.

Shiv Jayanti

shiv Jayanti is an annual Hindu festival celebrated to honor the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, a revered Maratha warrior king. It is observed with great enthusiasm in the Indian state of Maharashtra and other regions, with processions, cultural events, and prayers to pay homage to his legacy and leadership.

Gudi Padwa

Gudi Padwa, also known as Marathi New Year or Ugadi in other parts of India, is a significant Hindu festival that marks the beginning of the traditional Hindu lunar calendar. It is celebrated with the raising of a Gudi (a special flag-like structure) outside homes, feasting, and cultural festivities, symbolizing the start of a prosperous and auspicious year.
